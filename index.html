🎯 Investor Targeting

Not every investor is a fit. Targeting the wrong person wastes time and can damage your reputation in small networks.

📖 78% of VCs use "pattern matching" — know who you're approaching (Founders Forum, 2025)
1 What stage are you raising for?
2 Which sectors does your venture sit in?
3 Geography: where are your target investors based?
4 Are you intentionally targeting a mix of male and female investors?
⚠️ Research caution: Female founders who raise only from female VCs are twice as likely to struggle with follow-on funding — male investors must eventually be part of your mix. (Snellman & Solal, 2023)

🔗 Network Mapping

82% of VC deals come through warm introductions. Map who in your network can bridge you to target investors — and assess how credible that bridge is.

📖 Women are 38% less likely to have direct investor connections — warm intros must be actively built (Founders Forum, 2025)

A cold network means you need to build bridges first. Focus on connectors (advisors, accelerator alumni, professors) who can introduce you — don't cold-email investors directly. (Scheidgen & Brattström, 2023)

Vouching = they send a warm email saying "you should meet this founder" without you asking
